Conflict of Interest and Tariffs

LSA follows a strict “no conflict of interest” policy to avoid any potential or actual prejudice to its clients.

LSA’s fees and tariffs can be negotiated with each client to suit their needs. The importance, urgency and intricacy of the work are factors considered in determining fees and/or hourly tariffs.

Commercial matters agreements can be reached on a varied fee structure, depending on successful outcomes of transactions or otherwise.
